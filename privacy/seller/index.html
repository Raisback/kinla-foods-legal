Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kinla Seller ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of the limited data required to provide our services.

1. Nature of the Application

Kinla Seller is a business-to-business (B2B) tool designed specifically for registered food vendors. The app allows sellers to:

  • Manage and update their food menu items.
  • Receive incoming orders from customers.
  • Notify delivery riders when orders are ready for pickup.

2. Information Collection and Use

We believe in data minimization. Because this is a private management tool, our data collection practices are as follows:

  • No Self-Registration: User accounts are created manually by the system administrator. We do not collect registration data through the app.
  • No Automated Personal Data Collection: The app does not automatically collect your phone number, contacts, or precise GPS location.
  • Seller Content: We store the food titles, descriptions, and prices you input to display them to customers on the Kinla platform.

3. Order Data Flow

To fulfill orders, the app displays customer order details to you. This information is used strictly for food preparation and is shared with the designated Rider solely for the purpose of delivery. We do not use this data for marketing or third-party advertising.

4. Data Retention

We retain menu data and order history as long as your account is active to provide you with sales records and business continuity. If you wish to have your account or data deleted, please contact the administrator.

5. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect the information processed through the app. Access to the app is restricted to authorized sellers provided with manual login credentials.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
