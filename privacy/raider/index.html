Rider Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Kinla Foods Rider App. We value your privacy and aim to collect only the data necessary to facilitate efficient food delivery services.

A. Information We Collect

We keep data collection to a minimum. The following information is processed:

  • Authentication Data: We use your phone number and secure login credentials provided by the Kinla Foods administration to grant access to your rider profile.
  • Real-Time Location Data: To enable order assignments and allow customers to track their delivery, we collect your precise location data. This includes background location access while you are "Online" or on an active delivery, even if the app is minimized.
  • Device Information: Basic technical data (OS version, device model) to ensure the app functions correctly on your hardware.

B. How We Use Your Data

  • Order Logistics: To show your current position to the customer and the restaurant for real-time delivery tracking.
  • Route Optimization: To calculate the most efficient path for your deliveries within the Kinniya region.
  • Secure Communication: To allow you to call customers via WebRTC without exposing your personal phone number.

C. Background Location Disclosure

Note on Background Location: The Rider App collects location data to enable "Real-time Delivery Tracking" and "Order Proximity Alerts" even when the app is closed or not in use. Location collection stops immediately when you go "Offline" or log out.

D. Data Sharing

Your data is never sold. It is shared only with:

  • Customers: Who can see your location only while you are delivering their specific order.
  • Kinla Admin: To manage payroll, monitor delivery performance, and ensure safety.
  • Firebase/Google: For secure database hosting and real-time location updates.

E. Data Retention & Deletion

Location data is transient and is not stored permanently beyond the completion of the delivery cycle. You may request account deactivation by contacting the Kinla Foods Admin team or via the Support Page.
