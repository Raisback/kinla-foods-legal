Account Deletion & Data Removal

According to our commitment to your privacy, you can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion:

To request account deletion, please contact us via Email or WhatsApp (using the links above) with the following information:

  • Your registered Email address (Google Sign-in)
  • Your registered Phone Number
  • A brief mention that you wish to delete your Kinla Foods account

What happens next?

  • Data Deleted: Your profile information, order history, and saved delivery addresses will be permanently removed from our databases.
  • Retention: Some transaction data may be kept for a limited period solely for legal and tax compliance in Sri Lanka.
  • Processing Time: Most requests are processed within 48–72 hours.
